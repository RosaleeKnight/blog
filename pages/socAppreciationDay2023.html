SOC Appreciation Day 2023 | Oct 21, 2023

This was the first Security Conference I've attended which was the 3rd Annual SOC Appreciation on Day Oct 18. The conference was streamed live over 5 hours with chats for the participants. The recap is live on their site.

Agenda: SOC Hacks with John Hammond: Play by play on how different companies responded to the 3CX supply chain attack and worked together. CISOs in the Hot Seat: How CISO approach interacting with their teams and common questions like what skills do you need to become one. Mental Health Session with Cybermindz: The unique stressors SOC analyst face and the methodology used to deter it. Transitioning from Public to Private Sector Cyber: How to deal with the difference in work cultural values and how to present your gained knowledge and skills. A Day in the Life of SOC Analyst: Challenges and how work environments can improve. AI and Automation’s Role in the SOC: How AI can be used in a safer way and the dangers of using it for everything. There’s a Seat for Everyone in Cyber: Getting more diverse people into cybersecurity with programs and mentorship as well as how to deal with the glass ceilings still in place for them.

My favourite section was SOC Hacks with John Hammond. Hearing how someone would respond to a major incident and work with the vendor and other companies was gave me a good sense of how it's like when it's all hands-on deck. Besides the sessions the presenters collaborated to make Rock the SOC: A career guide for your first analyst role and beyond. This covers career paths and the continuous learning required. Suggested open-source tools to try, training platforms, news sources. How to leverage your LinkedIn. How to position yourself to get into leadership. It contains a lot of really good resources such as popular open-source tools like Burp Suite, training platforms like LetsDefend, and news outlets like Hacker News. Cybersecurity involves continuous learning and knowing what tool is used for what job so having an overview of tools and resources which has been vouched for is invaluable.
